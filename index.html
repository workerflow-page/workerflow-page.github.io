workerflow

https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9pfjph03g2nm?cid=git 
WorkerFlow is a transparent timeline calendar widget that permeates your desktop perfectly.
Intuitively understand your daily schedule in conjunction with Google Calendar, and maximize work productivity with your own custom scheduler.

WorkerFlow is a timeline widget that is fixed to the edge of a Windows screen.
Even if you don't open and close the calendar, the entire day's schedule always unfolds in front of you.

─────────────────────────────
■ Key Features
─────────────────────────────

[Google calendar is completely linked]
• Securely connect to your Google account with OAuth 2.0
• Automatic schedule synchronization (updated immediately at 5-minute intervals + app focus)
• Add, modify, or delete schedules within the app — Real-time reflection on Google Calendar
• 11 Google Calendar Colors Perfect Support
• Set notifications (10 minutes / 30 minutes / 1 hour ago), recurring schedule (daily / weekly / monthly / annual)

[Timeline Widget]
• Top/bottom/left/right of the screen — Secure to desired location
• Drag to adjust height/width freedom
• Drag to add a local schedule immediately
• Show Google schedules (Italic) and local schedules integrated into one timeline
• Current Time Live Line (Red Line)

[Monthly calendar view]
• View the full monthly schedule at a glance
• Click Date → Timeline Widget Immediately Switch to That Date
• Double-click → New Schedule Add Modal Open Immediately
• Drag to select a period and register a schedule

[UI / Customization]
• Apply Windows Micah Material — Translucent Background Effect
• Adjust background color and transparency
• Font size adjustment (10px to 30px)
• Always pin toggle on top
• Auto-run at Windows startup
• System Tray Resident — Click on the tray icon to show/hide quickly

[Multilingual support]
• Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, and Spanish ̃

─────────────────────────────
■ Notes
─────────────────────────────
• Google Account login is required on first run for Google Calendar link.
• If there is no Internet connection, only the local schedule is available.
• Support for Windows 10/11 (Mica effect only applies to Windows 11)
