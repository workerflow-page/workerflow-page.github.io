Developers value users' personal information and comply with related laws such as the "Act on Promotion of Information and Communication Network Utilization and Information Protection" and the "Personal Information Protection Act". This personal information processing policy informs how personal information provided is processed when users use the desktop application "WorkerFlow" (hereinafter referred to as "App").

### 1. Purpose of collecting and using personal information
The app does not collect and store personal information on the user's external server. Data is used only as a target system below for providing services.
- **Google Calendar interlocking and displaying schedules:** Process data only for the purpose of synchronizing users' Google calendar schedules with app screens (timelines, calendar widgets).

### 2. Items of personal information collected
The personal information items handled by the app are as follows.
- **Google account information and authentication data:** Google OAuth authentication token (Access token, Refresh token)
- **Calendar data: **Schedule title, time, content, etc. registered on users' Google calendar
- *** Important guide:** All collected data will not be transmitted to the developer's external database or server, and will only be stored and processed securely inside the user's PC (local storage).

### 3. Period of retention and use of personal information
- The app does not directly hold the user's personal information.
- If a user logs out a Google account from within the app or deletes (uninstalls) the application, the authentication tokens and schedule data stored on the local device are immediately destroyed in a non-archived state.

### 4. Provision and entrustment of personal information to third parties
Developers do not provide or entrust users' personal information to the outside world. The app communicates directly with Google servers through Google's official API, and data transmission that occurs in this process follows Google's privacy policy.

### 5. Compliance with Google User Data Policy
The use of information received by the app from Google API and its transfer to other apps is strictly in compliance with the [Google API Service User Data Policy] (including https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy)( restrictive usage requirements).

### 6. Users' rights and methods of exercise
Users can withdraw their consent to collect and use personal information by pressing the logout button in the app or deleting the app at any time.
